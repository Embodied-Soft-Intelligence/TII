Experiment Setup

Experimental Configuration

All experiments are conducted using a 6-DoF robotic manipulator equipped with a parallel gripper. An RGB-D camera is mounted to provide synchronized color and depth observations. The proposed framework performs object detection, manipulation relationship reasoning, grasp generation, and execution in a fully autonomous manner.

Real-World Robot Experiments

Real-world grasping experiments validate the effectiveness of the proposed method under stacked and cluttered scenarios without human intervention.

Stability Evaluation

The stability of stacked grasping is evaluated by measuring object collapse and disturbance propagation. Relationship-aware planning significantly improves grasp stability.

Robustness under Boundary Ambiguity

The method maintains robust grasping performance even when object boundaries are ambiguous, benefiting from manipulation relationship reasoning.
